# Cue Brand Policy

The source code in this repository is MIT-licensed, but the `Cue` name, icon, logo, store screenshots, review assets, and official listing copy are not part of that MIT grant.

Forks may study, modify, and redistribute the code.

Forks that publish binaries or store listings must rebrand.
They may not reuse:

- the `Cue` product name
- the official app icon or logo
- store screenshots or feature graphics
- official listing copy or review/demo creative
- the canonical package or bundle identity used by the official build

Redistributed forks must change the app name, bundle/package ID, iconography, and store metadata so users can clearly distinguish them from the official Cue release.
